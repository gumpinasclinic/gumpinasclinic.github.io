Common Questions

Frequently Asked
Questions

Everything you need to know before your visit.

Do I need a referral to see a specialist here? +
No referral is required. You can walk in or call us directly to book an appointment with any of our specialists. We welcome direct consultations.
What should I bring to my first endocrinology appointment? +
Please bring any previous blood test reports, medication lists, imaging reports (ultrasound, MRI, CT scans) and a list of your current symptoms. This helps us give you the most accurate assessment at the very first visit.
Do you see children for psychiatric and neurodevelopmental concerns? +
Yes. Dr. Deepthi Saka is certified in Child & Adolescent Psychiatry and regularly evaluates and manages children with ADHD, autism spectrum disorder, anxiety, developmental delays and other neurodevelopmental conditions.
Is the pharmacy stocked with newer diabetes medicines like tirzepatide? +
Our attached pharmacy stocks the latest diabetes medications including GLP-1 agonists (semaglutide), tirzepatide (Yurpeak), and modern insulin formulations. If a specific medication needs ordering, we can arrange it promptly.
Can I consult both the endocrinologist and psychiatrist on the same day? +
Yes, and we actively encourage this for patients with conditions like diabetes-related depression, PCOS with mood disorders, or metabolic syndrome with psychiatric comorbidities. Please call ahead to schedule both consultations.
What are the consultation fees? +
Please call us on +91 76761 06520 for current consultation fee details. We strive to keep our fees reasonable while offering the highest standard of specialist care.
Do you offer teleconsultation / online appointments? +
Yes, we offer teleconsultation for follow-up visits and review of reports. Please WhatsApp or call us to arrange a virtual appointment, especially for patients travelling from outside Vizianagaram.
How do I manage a diabetes emergency outside clinic hours? +
For urgent hypoglycaemia or hyperglycaemia, please proceed to the nearest emergency department immediately. For non-emergency after-hours queries, WhatsApp us and we will respond as soon as possible.
